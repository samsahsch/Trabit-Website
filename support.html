Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud is enabled, it syncs securely through your personal iCloud account. We never have access to your data.

Does Trabit work offline?

Yes. Trabit works fully offline. iCloud sync happens automatically when a connection is available.

How does Apple Health integration work?

When you grant permission, Trabit can read data like steps, sleep, and workouts from Apple Health to automatically track your habits. This data never leaves your device.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual habits or all data directly within the app. Since no data is stored on our servers, deleting it from your device removes it completely.
