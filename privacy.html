Privacy Policy for Trabit

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Trabit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the Trabit application.

1. Data Storage and Syncing

Trabit is designed with privacy as its core foundation. All of your habit tracking data, goals, and logs are stored locally on your device using Apple's native SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled, your data is securely synced across your devices using your personal Apple iCloud account (CloudKit). We do not own, operate, or maintain any external servers, and we do not have access to your personal data.

2. Apple Health (HealthKit) Integration

Trabit optionally integrates with Apple's Health app (HealthKit) to automatically log your habits (such as sleep, steps, or workout distances). If you explicitly grant Trabit permission to read this data, the following rules apply:

  • Read-Only Access: Trabit only reads the specific HealthKit data you authorize to calculate your habit progress.
  • No External Transmission: Your HealthKit data is processed entirely locally on your device. It is never transmitted to us or any third-party servers.
  • No Advertising or Marketing: We will never use your HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or any use-based data mining.
  • No Data Brokers: We will never sell, share, or disclose your HealthKit data to data brokers, advertising platforms, or other third parties.

3. Analytics and Tracking

Trabit does not include any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
