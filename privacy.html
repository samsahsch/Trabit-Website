Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Trabit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the Trabit application.

1. Data Storage

All of your habit tracking data, goals, and logs are stored locally on your device using Apple's native SwiftData framework. We do not own, operate, or maintain any external servers, and we do not have access to your personal habit data.

2. Social Features & Shared Data

Trabit offers optional social features that allow you to share goals and track progress with friends. When you opt in to these features, limited data is stored in Apple's CloudKit public database to enable sharing:

  • What is shared: Your display name (optional), share code, and any goals you explicitly choose to share — including the goal title, habit name, progress percentage, streak count, and completion status.
  • How it works: Sharing is powered entirely by Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. We do not operate a separate server.
  • Your control: You choose which goals to share on a per-goal basis. You can stop sharing any goal at any time. No data is shared unless you explicitly enable it.
  • Friend connections: Friend links are stored in your private iCloud database and are only visible to you.
  • HealthKit data is never shared: Your Apple Health data is never included in shared goals, even when social features are enabled.

3. Apple Health (HealthKit) Integration

Trabit optionally integrates with Apple's Health app (HealthKit) to automatically log habits such as steps, distance, and workouts. If you grant permission:

  • Read-Only Access: Trabit only reads the specific HealthKit data you authorize to calculate your habit progress.
  • No External Transmission: Your HealthKit data is processed entirely locally on your device. It is never transmitted to us or any third-party servers.
  • No Advertising or Marketing: We will never use your HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or any use-based data mining.
  • No Data Brokers: We will never sell, share, or disclose your HealthKit data to data brokers, advertising platforms, or other third parties.

4. Analytics and Tracking

The Trabit app does not include any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites.

The trabit.app website uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly, open-source analytics tool that collects anonymous page view statistics. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and is fully GDPR compliant.

5. On-Device AI

Trabit uses Apple's on-device AI (FoundationModels framework) to parse natural language input for habit logging. All AI processing happens locally on your device. No input or data is sent to external servers for processing.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@trabit.app
